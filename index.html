Welcome

Sahra Qadi is a passionate student of design and digital media who explores the intersections of storytelling, activism, and visual communication.

She is particularly focused on issues affecting women in Somalia, using creative platforms to amplify voices and advocate for change.

“Design is a tool for empathy, activism, and change.” – Sahra Qadi

What inspires your work?

My work is driven by stories of resilience and the need to represent unheard voices through media and design.

Where do you study?

I’m currently pursuing Design and Digital Media at CUNY.
